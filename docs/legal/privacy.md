# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to dev.hector.pantry ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs the data collection and usage within our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:
- **Account Information:** When you authenticate using Google Sign-In, we receive your email address and basic profile information provided by the identity provider.
- **Pantry Data:** Information about the products you scan, add to your pantry, and your consumption history. This data is synchronized to our cloud servers (Supabase) to provide multi-device access.
- **Camera Access:** The App requests access to your device's camera exclusively for scanning product barcodes. We do not capture, record, or upload continuous video feeds or photos of your surroundings. The camera is active only when the scanner screen is explicitly opened by the user.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To manage your account and provide the core functionality of the App (pantry management, expiration tracking, price history).
- To synchronize your offline data (stored locally in SQLite) with your cloud account.
- We **do not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertising networks.

## 4. Local and Cloud Storage
- **Offline-First Storage:** Your pantry data is stored locally on your device using SQLite to ensure the App functions without an internet connection.
- **Cloud Synchronization:** When online, your local data is synchronized securely with our cloud infrastructure hosted on Supabase. This infrastructure complies with modern encryption standards both in transit and at rest.

## 5. User Rights and Data Deletion
Under the GDPR and other privacy frameworks, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the App's Settings screen via the "Delete Account Permanently" option.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official repository or support channels.
